  • had 5,000 vacuum tubes
  • weighed 8 tons
  • occupied 382 sq ft
  • consumed 125 kW
  • 1000 words (12 characters) of mercury delay line memory
  • 10 tape drives, 1 M characters each
  • 1,905 operations per second running on a 2.25 MHz clock.
